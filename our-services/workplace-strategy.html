Expertise / Workplace strategy

Workplace strategy turns business ambition into a clear spatial brief.

We help leadership teams understand how the organisation works today, what needs to change, and how the future workplace should support people, operations, culture, and growth.

Strategy connects the evidence on the table with the reality of the workplace: how people meet, focus, move, share space, and make the office useful again.

Scope

A clear path from current reality to project direction.

01
Space analysis

We study the existing workplace, capacity, adjacencies, occupancy patterns, constraints, and how the office is actually used day to day.

02
Organisational goals, workflows and culture

We clarify how teams work, what leadership wants to achieve, and which cultural behaviours the workplace needs to make easier.

03
Operational challenges and transformation opportunities

We identify friction points, underused areas, future risks, and moments where a different workplace model can improve performance.

04
Project definition and stakeholder alignment

We establish the project ambition, decision logic, priorities, and shared understanding needed before design, budget, and implementation move forward.

Method

From insight to a usable brief.

We keep the process simple: understand the organisation, read how the workplace performs, then turn the evidence into clear decisions for the next stage.

Understand

Leadership interviews, stakeholder input, workplace observations, and operational context.

Interpret

Space use, workflows, constraints, behaviours, pressure points, and opportunities for change.

Direct

Shared priorities, decision criteria, project ambition, and the brief that guides the next stage.
