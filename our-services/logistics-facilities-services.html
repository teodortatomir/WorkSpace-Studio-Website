Logistics & Facilities Services

24h acknowledgment. 72h guaranteed response.

In-house warehousing, just-in-time delivery and installation during the office project. Day-2 service after handover, run by the same trusted team.

Logistics & Facilities Services covers two distinct phases of every office project at Workspace Studio: the build phase (warehousing, just-in-time delivery, installation) and the post-handover phase (Day-2 service, warranty handling, reconfigurations, maintenance).

The same in-house logistics team handles both. A 6,000 m² Workspace Studio warehouse stores furniture, architectural products and site materials, with sequencing tuned to the project programme to minimise on-site storage and disruption to ongoing operations.

After handover, the team stays on under a 24h acknowledgment / 72h guaranteed response SLA, covering everything from a single chair replacement to floor-level reconfigurations as the organisation evolves.

What happens during the build phase

Warehousing, delivery and installation move in the same rhythm as the project programme.

01

In-house warehousing

Workspace Studio operates a 6,000 m² dedicated warehouse for office furniture, architectural products and project materials. Warehousing in-house means the project programme drives delivery sequencing, with no dependency on third-party logistics windows or storage limits on the client's site.

02

Just-in-time delivery

Materials and furniture arrive on site in the sequence the construction programme needs them. Workstations land after partitions and flooring are complete; meeting room furniture follows AV integration; lounge zones are sequenced for the final phase. Storage on the active fit-out site stays minimal, which reduces site clutter and shortens snagging.

03

In-house installation

Furniture and architectural products are installed by Workspace Studio's directly employed logistics and installation teams, the same teams responsible for Day-2 service after handover. The chain of accountability from delivery to commissioning runs through one team.

Day-2 services

What happens after move-in day

After handover, the relationship with Workspace Studio continues under a single Day-2 service program. The same team that built the office stays accountable, with consistent response standards across all post-handover requests.

24h acknowledgment / 72h guaranteed response

Every service request from an existing client receives acknowledgment within 24 hours and a substantive response within 72 hours. The SLA covers everything from product warranty claims to operational reconfigurations.

Warranty handling

Manufacturer warranties on furniture and architectural products are managed directly by Workspace Studio's team, with the manufacturer relationships and certified-dealer status that allow direct resolution. Clients deal with one point of contact through the entire warranty period.

Reconfigurations

Organisations change. Teams grow, restructure, shift between hybrid arrangements, move floors. Workspace Studio handles partial reconfigurations (a new team zone, a converted meeting room, an additional 50 workstations, or a complete floor change after restructuring) using the same specifications and brands installed originally, so the office reads as one continuous design over time.

Maintenance and operational support

Furniture maintenance, partition adjustments, signage updates, replacement parts and the operational housekeeping that an active corporate office generates over time.

In-house infrastructure

Physical capacity, operational tooling and showroom support under one Workspace Studio system.

Logistics capability sits on physical infrastructure that Workspace Studio operates directly. The 6,000 m² warehouse handles project materials and furniture inventory, with capacity tuned for projects from 100 m² boutique offices to 30,000 m² corporate headquarters running in parallel.

Continuous investment in technology and process supports the operation. Over €300,000 invested in 2025 alone in tracking systems, inventory management, operational tooling and integration with the active project pipeline, with the result of shorter delivery cycles and lower friction during installation phases.

The 850 m² Workspace Studio showroom in Sky Tower, Bucharest, expanded in 2023, supports client visits, product specification sessions, live ergonomic assessments and project-stage workshops alongside the logistics operation.
