Design & Build

Turn key solutions for all project scales

One in-house team handles workplace strategy, design, architecture, technical engineering and fit-out execution. One contract, one schedule, one accountable point of contact.

Integrated delivery model

Design, technical scope, procurement and site delivery under one chain of accountability.

Design & Build is Workspace Studio's integrated delivery model for corporate office projects. A single in-house team handles workplace strategy, architectural design, technical engineering, MEP coordination, procurement and site execution, under one contract.

The model removes coordination cost between separate vendors and consolidates accountability into one chain. The budget is validated against technical scope before procurement begins and held through delivery.

Projects range from 100 m² boutique offices to 30,000 m² corporate headquarters, delivered across Romania for clients such as OMV Petrom, L'Oréal, Adobe, UiPath and 700+ other companies.

The sole MillerKnoll Certified Dealership in Romania anchors the furniture offer, while direct partnerships with 500+ workplace brands cover the rest.

Project steps

How a Design & Build project moves from brief to handover

01

Brief and workplace diagnostic

We start with operational priorities, headcount projections, working patterns and budget range. Where needed, we run space analysis, stakeholder alignment and a written project brief before design begins.

02

Concept design and cost validation

Architects produce concept design, space planning and 3D visualisations alongside the cost team. The budget is validated against technical scope before the project moves forward.

03

Technical design and procurement

Architectural, MEP and furniture specifications are finalised in coordinated drawings. Procurement starts with framework agreements and direct manufacturer pricing.

04

Site execution

Fit-out works are run by Workspace Studio's in-house teams. Partitions, ceilings, flooring, MEP integration, joinery and finishes are coordinated by one site manager on one schedule.

05

Handover and Day-2 service

Snagging, commissioning, documentation and key handover. The same team stays on for warranty handling, reconfigurations and maintenance through Logistics & Facilities Services.

Budget Centricity

Cost certainty, embedded from the startin your office Design & Build project

In typical office fit-out projects, cost overruns happen because cost analysis comes after design is approved. Workspace Studio reverses this sequence. Cost lives inside design, not after it.

Cost-aligned concept design

Every concept is checked against budget before it moves to technical design. Designs that exceed the budget get revised at concept stage, before procurement begins.

In-house cost team

Quantity surveyors work alongside architects from day one. Value engineering happens during design, when changes are inexpensive.

Locked budget at sign-off

The budget agreed at contract signing holds through delivery. No revisions under cost pressure mid-project. No surprise change orders at handover.

The result: predictability for CFOs and procurement teams who need to defend the investment internally, with cost certainty as a contractual commitment rather than an aspiration.
