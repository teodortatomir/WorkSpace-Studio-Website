Architectural Products & Solutions

Architectural Products & Solutions

Flooring, partitions, acoustic solutions, window treatments and signage that define how the office looks, sounds and reconfigures over time.

Workspace Studio specifies, supplies and coordinates these products alongside the fit-out execution, sourced from direct manufacturer partnerships across Europe, North America and selected Asian suppliers.

Direct supply removes intermediary margins and gives access to manufacturer pricing, original product warranty and the technical specification support that complex office projects need.

Products are coordinated by Workspace Studio's in-house design and procurement teams against the architectural drawings, the MEP plan and the project programme. The same logistics team that handles furniture installation runs the delivery and on-site coordination.

Flooring

Flooring

Flooring covers more than 20-50% of any office surface and shapes acoustics, wayfinding, zoning and the perceived quality of the space. Carpet tiles handle high-traffic areas with replaceable modularity; LVT and resilient flooring deliver durability and easy maintenance for café and circulation zones; bespoke carpet inserts mark out leadership areas and collaboration zones. Acoustic underlays reduce footfall transmission in open-plan offices and below meeting rooms.

Workspace Studio specifies and supplies flooring from direct manufacturer partnerships with Mohawk, Interface, Forbo and other contract-flooring brands. Installation is run by specialist subcontractors under Workspace Studio's site management. Existing floor surveys, substrate preparation and acoustic detailing are coordinated as part of the technical design phase.

Partitions and flexible walls

Partitions and flexible walls

Partitioning systems define how an office uses its floorplate: enclosed offices, meeting rooms, phone rooms, training spaces, breakout zones, plus the boundary between collaboration and focused work.

Drywall partitions remain the standard for permanent enclosure, while demountable glass walls allow reconfiguration without construction work and bring daylight into the floor plate; modular partition systems support fast layout changes as teams scale.

Workspace Studio supplies partition systems from MillerKnoll's Maars Living Walls, plus other European demountable wall manufacturers and drywall systems.

Specification accounts for acoustic performance (typically Rw 35-45 dB for meeting rooms), fire rating, integration with MEP and AV, and the level of future reconfiguration the client expects.

Acoustic solutions

Acoustic solutions

Open-plan offices generate the largest single complaint in workplace surveys: noise. Acoustic solutions cover ceiling panels, wall absorbers, free-standing screens, decorative acoustic elements and high-performance ceiling systems. Specification responds to the floor's reverberation profile, the density of seating, the proximity of meeting rooms to focused-work zones, and the type of work that needs to happen in each space.

Office pods (acoustic phone booths and meeting pods) sit at the intersection of acoustic and furniture. Workspace Studio supplies them as part of the integrated offer, with Framery as anchor brand alongside other manufacturers. See the Furniture sub-page for the full pod range.

Workspace Studio works directly with acoustic solutions suppliers like Impact Acoustic, Abstracta, Buzzispace and other contract acoustics manufacturers. Acoustic targets are validated against the project brief during technical design and verified post-installation.

Window blinds & curtains

Window blinds & Curtains

Blinds, curtains, motorised shades and solar films control glare, heat gain and visual privacy on the office facade. Specification depends on facade orientation, glazing performance, the role of each space (boardrooms typically need full blackout for AV; open-plan zones need solar control with daylight transmission) and integration with BMS where applicable.

Workspace Studio supplies window blinds and curtains from European manufacturers, with motorised options integrated into the BMS during MEP coordination. Site survey, control specification and installation are run by the in-house team.

Signage and architectural finishes

Signage and architectural finishes

Signage covers wayfinding, room identification, brand expression at reception and visitor-facing zones, plus regulatory signage (fire safety, accessibility, evacuation).

Architectural finishes include feature walls, metal cladding, decorative surfaces, custom millwork and the elements that translate brand identity into the physical space.

Workspace Studio coordinates signage and architectural finishes with the broader fit-out, supplied from specialist Romanian and European manufacturers. Custom architectural elements are produced in collaboration with our in-house joinery and external specialist workshops, depending on scope.
