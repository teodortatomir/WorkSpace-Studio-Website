Who we are

100% Romanian-owned.Run by its founders since 2008.

A leading workplace transformation company built in Romania, delivering corporate offices to a premium standard.

Brand philosophy

Workspace Studio was built on a simple belief: the office is an investment in the people who work in it. A well-designed workplace shapes how people feel, how they collaborate, how long they stay and how an organisation performs. That belief has guided every project since 2008.

As a 100% Romanian-owned company run by its founders, Workspace Studio makes decisions locally and quickly, with none of the layers an international head office adds. Ownership sits inside daily operations, so accountability sits with the people who answer the phone.

Mission.

To design and deliver offices that help people work better and organisations perform better, at a premium standard, from concept through to the years after handover.

Vision.

To be the benchmark for corporate workplaces in Romania, the partner organisations turn to when the office has to work for the people in it.

Values.

Partnership: staying involved through the whole project and the years that follow it.

Client orientation: designing around each organisation's real needs and culture.

Quality: a standard maintained from the first concept onward.

Our workplace approach

Every Workspace Studio project runs on the same four principles, from the first cost estimate to the service years after handover.

Together they hold one idea in place: the workplace is an investment in the people who use it.

Quality

Quality is one standard, held from the first concept through every later phase. One in-house team controls scope and detailing end to end, which closes the quality gaps that open between separate vendors. The same team runs Day-2 service after handover, so that standard stays in place for years.

Human-centred design

We design corporate interiors around how an organisation actually works. Working patterns, team structure, social dynamics and cultural context are mapped before any design begins, so the layout reflects the organisation behind it. Engagement and long-term retention become design outcomes, anchored in MillerKnoll research and recent global studies.

Budget centricity

Cost discipline sits inside the design process from the first concept. The in-house cost team works alongside the architects, every concept validated against the budget before technical design begins. The budget is locked at contract signing and held through delivery, so clients get cost certainty before the first commitment on site.

Ergonomics

Ergonomics is written into the brief from the start. It runs across three dimensions: physical ergonomics at the workstation and the wider environment, social ergonomics in how the floor plan supports focus and exchange, and inclusive ergonomics for the full diversity of a modern workforce. As MillerKnoll Certified Dealer in Romania, the seating offer anchors on Herman Miller and the iconic Aeron.

In-house delivery

Core delivery stays in-house. 70+ directly employed specialists cover every phase: architects, engineers, project managers, logisticians and installers. The same accountability runs from design to the Day-2 service that follows handover. The team also trains continuously alongside MillerKnoll, bringing global workplace research into the practical work of specification and installation.

MillerKnoll: an ecosystem of knowledge

As the sole MillerKnoll Certified Dealer in Romania, Workspace Studio is connected to the world's largest workplace furniture collective and the research engine behind it. MillerKnoll reported net sales of $3.7 billion in FY2025 and brings Herman Miller and Knoll together with a wider group of heritage design brands, under continuous research in ergonomics, workplace performance, sustainable manufacturing and materials.

The certification brings direct access to MillerKnoll research and to the specification expertise built on it. Ergonomic standards, post-occupancy studies, workplace performance data and the design science that shapes how the products evolve feed into how each project is specified.

The collective's design heritage runs from the modernist work of Charles and Ray Eames, Florence Knoll, Eero Saarinen and George Nelson to the research that now studies how people actually use the workspace. For a Romanian project, that means design decisions backed by global evidence, quality and future forward studies.

Certifications

Workspace Studio holds independently audited certifications.

  • ISO 9001 for quality management
  • ISO 14001 for environmental management
  • ISO 45001 for occupational health and safety
  • EcoVadis business sustainability rating.

Together they cover how the work is delivered, how its environmental impact is managed, how people are kept safe on site, and how the business is assessed against external sustainability criteria. At project level, Workspace Studio has delivered corporate interiors certified to standards such as LEED, BREEAM and WELL, where the client's building or sustainability targets call for them.

For procurement and compliance teams, recognised certifications reduce vendor onboarding and the audit overhead that office fit-out contracts usually carry. One certified supplier replaces several agreements to negotiate, monitor and renew.
