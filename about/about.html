About / Workspace Studio

A Romanian workplace company shaped by design culture, accountability, and global know-how.

Workspace Studio creates high-quality workplaces through human-oriented design, budget discipline, ergonomics, and teams that stay close to every project from first idea to handover.

Brand Philosophy

100% Romanian, built around trust, quality, and workplaces that genuinely support people.

Founded in Bucharest in 2008, Workspace Studio is a Romanian company with an international standard of work. Our mission is to create offices that help organisations perform better while making daily work healthier, clearer, and more human.

We believe a workplace should be beautiful, but never superficial. It has to respect budget, support the body, simplify collaboration, and stay durable long after the opening day.

Mission

To design and deliver workplaces that improve performance, wellbeing, and long-term business value.

Beliefs

People, quality, budget, ergonomics, and implementation discipline must be considered together.

Core values

Accountability, clarity, craft, partnership, responsible investment, and respect for the user.

Romanian roots

A local company with direct responsibility, fast coordination, and deep knowledge of the Romanian market.

Our Workplace Approach

A workplace is judged by how well it supports the day.

Team & Know-how

Senior involvement, in-house capability, and direct accountability.

Workspace Studio is led by shareholders who remain actively involved in the work. That keeps quality, responsibility, and client decisions close to the people accountable for the final result.
