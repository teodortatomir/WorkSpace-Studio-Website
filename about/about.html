About Workspace Studio

Spaces shaped with clarity, culture, and long-term performance.

Workspace Studio creates workplaces that feel refined, human, and deeply aligned with how teams actually work, meet, focus, and grow.

Who We Are

Design & build thinking with a premium workplace lens.

Founded in 2008, Workspace Studio has developed a practice built around workplace strategy, design, furniture specification, turnkey fit-out coordination, and ergonomics. The studio pairs concept quality with practical delivery so the end result feels elevated, coherent, and ready for everyday use.

Our work brings together office interiors, architectural systems, furniture, and brand-led detail into a single experience, with a curated network of global manufacturers and a strong implementation mindset.

2008 Long-term design & build practice in Bucharest.
100+ International brands, manufacturers, and workplace partners.
MillerKnoll Certified dealer and strategic premium furniture partner.
Sky Tower Showroom and client meeting point in Bucharest.
Studio Approach

Spatial clarity, tactile materials, and a process that keeps quality intact through every project stage.

We shape environments that feel calm and elevated, but are also highly practical. That means balancing architecture, furniture, ergonomics, circulation, and visual identity into one coherent workplace story.
