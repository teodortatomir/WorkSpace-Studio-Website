Press release / Bucharest, February 2022

Workspace Studio Group reached EUR 10 million in revenue in 2021.

Office fit-outs in 2021: smaller total areas, fewer workstations and higher quality.

Press releaseBack to press releases

Workspace Studio Group, which includes Workspace Studio and Allspace Interiors, companies specialized in ergonomic turnkey office fit-out solutions and Herman Miller's only accredited partner in Romania, closed last year with revenue of EUR 10 million.

The figure remained at the same level as the previous year, against a backdrop of caution in the office fit-out segment. Changes in the labor market, especially the broad adoption of remote work, supported sales to the residential segment, which continued the growth trend started in 2020.

In 2021, organizations invested on average EUR 1,500 per workstation in office fit-outs, while investment in an ergonomic home office rose to as much as EUR 2,000.

Workplace design in 2021 and trends for 2022

Alongside the market-level shift in approach, companies are investing in fit-outs and refurbishments in order to gradually return to office work. In 2021, 90% of Workspace Studio projects were small and medium-sized projects adapted for teams of 50-200 people.

Most projects were for spaces between 300 and 1,000 square meters, with total project budgets between EUR 100,000 and EUR 500,000. The average value of fit-out projects in 2021 was EUR 300,000, with an average investment of EUR 450 per square meter, around 20% higher than pre-pandemic years and influenced by accelerated inflation.

The quality level of office fit-outs continues to rise as total surfaces have decreased by around 30%. The area occupied by individual workstations has also fallen, representing around 40% of total surface compared with 70-80% before the pandemic. The remaining space is dedicated to multifunctional common areas that support collaboration, socialization and mentoring.

The changes offices are going through are both an evolution and an adaptation to the digital era. The pandemic accelerated the transformation of offices from places for completing tasks into destinations for collaboration, teamwork and even enjoyable activities that contribute to cohesion and employer branding. The effect is airier office spaces, with lively and attractive design, high-quality furniture and superior physical and social ergonomics, says Horatiu Didea.

Workspace Studio also noticed growing interest in premium furniture pieces that add elegance to offices and contribute to a positive perception of the organization's brand among employees and visitors. As competition for talent intensifies, the office becomes more exclusive and a business card for the company.

Remote work as a catalyst for residential sales

In the context of remote work, still predominant for many employees, Workspace Studio continued investing in the product portfolio addressed to the residential segment and ended 2021 with positive momentum. B2C sales generated EUR 700,000 in revenue, up 15% compared with the previous year.

Accelerated digitalization and work from home or hybrid work brought negative consequences for postural health, increasingly understood by employees who work remotely. Two years later, more Romanians consider it essential for productivity to set up a dedicated, ergonomic and high-quality workspace at home, says Horatiu Didea.

Investment in an ergonomic home office ranged on average between EUR 1,000 and EUR 2,000, with preferred pieces including Herman Miller ergonomic chairs, monitor arms and electric height-adjustable desks.

Top B2B versus B2C sales

Herman Miller ergonomic chair sales generate 30% of Workspace Studio's business. Among the most sought-after and best-selling products in 2021 were Aeron, Embody and Mirra chairs by Herman Miller, global leaders in ergonomics that provide personalized adjustment based on individual physical characteristics.

The online store's best-selling products were Aeron and Embody chairs for the residential segment, while the B2B segment was led by the Ratio adjustable desk, Aeron and Mirra chairs.

About Workspace Studio

Workspace Studio distributes high-quality ergonomic office furniture and is Herman Miller's sole Authorized Dealer Partner in Romania. Its interior design solutions are personalized and integrated turnkey, with an emphasis on ergonomics, financial sustainability and human-centric design.
