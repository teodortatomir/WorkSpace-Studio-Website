Press release / Bucharest, October 2020

Workspace Studio reached EUR 6.5 million in the first nine months.

The company enters the retail market with an online shop dedicated to ergonomic products.

Press releaseBack to press releases

Workspace Studio, a company specialized in workplace solutions focused on ergonomics and Herman Miller's only accredited partner in Romania, announces its entry into online retail after a EUR 100,000 investment in the platform and dedicated initial stock. In the first nine months of this year, Workspace Studio recorded revenue of EUR 6.5 million.

The current context and the growth in demand for ergonomic furniture in the residential segment over the past six months led Workspace Studio to expand into retail with an online store that includes the most requested products for this customer segment. In the first stage, the online shop offer includes Herman Miller ergonomic chairs, products for which interest grew by 300% between March and September 2020.

The company aims for the online store to generate sales of more than EUR 100,000 by the end of the year. As many employees continue to work from home, interest in ergonomic chairs keeps growing, driven by the need to set up a comfortable and ergonomic workspace at home in order to maintain productivity and health.

The new reality of the labor market offers a clear perspective: employees who work remotely need the comfort of the office. Whether we are talking about the chair, the work table, lighting or other elements, they cannot perform in an improvised space. This is also reflected in requests from companies that have traditionally been employee-oriented and have offered budgets for setting up a comfortable workspace at home, says Horatiu Didea, Managing Partner Workspace Studio.

Free eBook for setting up the home office

To support customers, Workspace Studio created an eBook with advice and solutions for setting up a home office, so that productivity and physical health are not affected when remote work extends over a longer period. The guide can be downloaded free of charge.

It includes recommendations related to ambience, including space, acoustics and lighting, as well as posture, suitable office furniture and the way work, breaks, meetings and many other routines should be organized.

A bet on positive retail momentum, supported by work from home

The investment in expanding toward B2C was EUR 100,000, directed toward the online platform and the initial stock of Herman Miller chairs dedicated to this audience segment. This continues the product portfolio development direction aimed at the residential segment that Workspace Studio began in the first half of this year, when it introduced more than 200 HAY furniture products.

Work from home will remain predominant for a significant share of employees, and in the long term they need ergonomic workstations at home in order to remain productive. We estimate that in three years the B2C segment will generate around EUR 1 million in annual revenue and represent a significant share of Workspace Studio's total operations, says Horatiu Didea.

About Workspace Studio

Workspace Studio distributes high-quality ergonomic office furniture and is Herman Miller's sole Authorized Dealer Partner in Romania.

The solutions proposed by Workspace are personalized and integrated turnkey, with an emphasis on ergonomics, financial sustainability and design centered on people, their activities and their interactions.

As one of the few players in the market oriented toward human-centric design, Workspace Studio is Herman Miller's Authorized Dealer Partner in Romania. Herman Miller, the world's most prestigious office furniture manufacturer, creates innovative solutions that help people do extraordinary things in their work. Herman Miller concepts and the designers who work with Herman Miller solve real problems for people and organizations around the world.

This design approach has made Herman Miller recognized as a global innovation leader in office furniture, accessories and strategic ergonomic services. Herman Miller products are displayed in the permanent collections of major museums around the world.
