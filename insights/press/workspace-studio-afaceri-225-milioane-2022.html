Press release / February 2023

Workspace Studio reached EUR 22.5 million in revenue in 2022.

The growth came from premium solutions for high-quality office fit-outs.

Press releaseBack to press releases

Workspace Studio Group, specialized in high-quality office design and fit-outs and a MillerKnoll Certified Dealer in Romania, increased its revenue last year to EUR 22.5 million.

The positive momentum was supported by companies' need to offer employees attractive office spaces, together with Workspace Studio's strategy of developing its portfolio and integrating the highest-quality ergonomic furniture, acoustic, partitioning and flooring solutions into fit-out projects, with a strong emphasis on design.

Workspace Studio's development is based on the premise that spaces fitted out to high-quality standards are becoming a necessity recognized by more and more organizations. In the premium segment the market is growing, and our portfolio of solutions and services is built to meet the expectations of comfort, ergonomics, design and sustainability of modern offices, says Horatiu Didea.

The new meaning of office spaces

Compared with 2021, the number of medium-sized projects, with values between EUR 250,000 and EUR 400,000, doubled last year. More than half involved rethinking space on an area up to 25% smaller, but with substantial investment in quality. The focus on higher quality, ergonomics and design was also visible in large projects worth more than EUR 1 million.

The average budget allocated, including the individual workstation and its share of common areas, increased to over EUR 2,000 per workstation as a result of integrating furniture solutions with superior ergonomics and design.

The area occupied by individual desks fell below 45% of total surface, while emphasis moved toward collaboration, socializing and relaxation spaces, which rose toward 60% in most projects.

As competition for talent intensifies across many sectors, the office becomes more exclusive and also a business card for the company, as well as a communication tool for organizational values and culture. The office is also developing into a functional catalyst for social work processes: idea and know-how exchange, learning, mentoring, teamwork, creation and innovation.

2022 was a year of repositioning, in which companies evaluated the effects of pandemic changes, made decisions about future work models and acted to create office spaces that support current needs. Fit-outs now aim to transform offices into destinations where teams want to meet and have pleasant experiences that cannot be lived remotely, explains Horatiu Didea.

Spaces that offer on-demand privacy and areas for focus or individual work, such as acoustic booths, addressed employees' need for optimal work conditions and appeared in more than 90% of 2022 projects.

2023: focus on ergonomics, design and sustainability

Although each workspace must be built around the organization's culture and the specifics of work, several directions are recurrent. The main post-pandemic trends are emphasis on ergonomics and design, flexible space through partitioning solutions with superior sound insulation and technical performance, acoustic solutions such as acoustic booths and decorative acoustic elements, and the sustainability of solutions integrated into interior architecture.

Starting in 2022, we add design and sustainability to our focus on service quality and ergonomics. These two directions will change the face and philosophy of fit-outs, both in offices and in premium and luxury residential projects. They contributed to doubling revenue last year and will continue to support the company's performance, adds Horatiu Didea.

Workspace Studio's architectural product portfolio has developed to answer the needs of modern spaces from both design and functionality perspectives. By working constantly with high-quality suppliers, Workspace Studio integrated some of the most performant market solutions into its projects, bringing a high degree of efficiency and sustainability.

When you think of a project as a long-term investment, the quality and durability of solutions are essential. Partition systems introduced in current projects allow later space reconfiguration with minimal effort. Acoustic solutions made from recycled, biodegradable or natural materials reduce background noise and make the office more pleasant, while products with warranties of more than 10 years justify the investment through quality and lifespan, says Horatiu Didea.

About Workspace Studio

Workspace Studio distributes high-quality ergonomic office furniture and architectural products and is the sole MillerKnoll Certified Dealer in Romania. Its turnkey interior design solutions are personalized and integrated, with an emphasis on ergonomics, sustainability and human-centric design.

Workspace Studio Group, which includes Workspace Studio and Allspace Interiors, specializes in ergonomic turnkey office fit-out solutions.
