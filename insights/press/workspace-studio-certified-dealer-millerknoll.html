Workspace Studio becomes a MillerKnoll Certified Dealer.

The company expands its portfolio of premium fit-out brands and targets revenue of over EUR 11 million this year.

Press releaseBack to press releases

Following the merger of two iconic design names, Knoll and Herman Miller, Workspace Studio becomes a Certified Dealer of the MillerKnoll group in Romania. Workspace Studio expands its portfolio with almost 20 design brands and offers a complete range of premium and ergonomic furniture and fit-out solutions.

With this expanded range, Workspace Studio addresses the market interested in quality pieces across all verticals, from office projects to complex commercial and luxury residential projects. For this year, Workspace Studio Group targets revenue of more than EUR 11 million, up over 10% compared with 2021.

A bet on premium design across office, residential and commercial segments

Since the start of the pandemic, office fit-outs have changed considerably, moving toward functionality, space reconversion and the creation of collaboration areas. Comfortable and less formal spaces that stimulate creativity, innovation, mentoring and idea exchange borrow elements from residential and lounge design, such as sofas, armchairs, coffee tables and decorative objects.

Smaller spaces, with greater focus on design and quality

The return of employees to offices is supported by organizations through higher-quality fit-outs, a trend observed since previous years. In 2022, both the area allocated to employees and the appetite for premium statement furniture pieces are increasing.

While total office surfaces have decreased by around 25%, fit-outs have been redesigned for a smaller number of employees present at the office as a result of the widespread adoption of hybrid work. Average surfaces per employee have increased to 13-15 square meters, compared with around 10 square meters before the pandemic, while the average budget has reached over EUR 1,800 per workstation, up 20% compared with 2021.

Individual work areas represent approximately 40% of total office surface, while more than half is occupied by common, multifunctional spaces. These can serve both formal and informal roles, including relaxation areas, now essential in the 2022 office as wellbeing becomes increasingly integrated into employees' lives.

Most projects in the first part of this year had three essential characteristics: ergonomics, premium design and the blending of classic residential elements into the office, and office elements into the residential space. As a MillerKnoll certified dealer, we can efficiently integrate luxury pieces with iconic design from Knoll and Herman Miller alongside quality pieces with accessible prices from HAY, Muuto and many other brands, says Horatiu Didea.

Premium design on all market segments

With easier access to the suite of furniture and fit-out brands within the MillerKnoll group, Workspace Studio expects growing adoption of quality design across all verticals of the interior design market. The residential segment, fueled by remote work over the last two years, currently generates 5-7% of group revenue. By the end of next year, premium and luxury commercial and residential fit-outs are expected to generate 10-15% of total revenue.

Regardless of socio-economic circumstances, quality design will continue to grow in Romania. People want remarkable, high-quality pieces, not only as a classic investment because they retain value, but as an investment in themselves, in comfort and wellbeing, says Horatiu Didea.

The 17 names united under the MillerKnoll umbrella represent more than 100 years of design research, each bringing a unique perspective and a diverse, versatile product range with a durable and sustainable approach across residential, office and commercial domains.

Among the brands represented by Workspace Studio starting in June are Knoll, Muuto, Maharam, Geiger and Edelman, joining Herman Miller, HAY, NaughtOne, Framery and many others. The portfolio includes ergonomic desks and chairs, sofas, armchairs, terrace and garden furniture, lighting, partitions, acoustic solutions, phone booths, rugs, curtains, wallpaper and storage furniture.

There is growing demand in Romania for premium furniture, both for office fit-outs and residential projects. For us, this means we can provide complete fit-out services for offices, commercial spaces and luxury residential projects, adds Horatiu Didea.

About Workspace Studio

Workspace Studio distributes high-quality ergonomic office furniture and is the sole MillerKnoll Certified Dealer in Romania. Workspace Studio Group, which includes Workspace Studio and Allspace Interiors, closed 2021 with revenue of EUR 10 million.
