Workspace Studio reaches EUR 27 million in 2024, with more than 100,000 sqm of modern offices fitted out and furnished.

The company reports 20% growth compared with the previous year, supported by large projects and collaborations with medium and small companies.

Press releaseBack to press releases

Workspace Studio, a leader in ergonomic fit-out solutions and employee-centered design, announces solid results for 2024, with revenue of EUR 27 million, up 20% compared with the previous year. The success was supported by large projects for top companies, as well as numerous collaborations with medium and small companies, all benefiting from the same orientation toward superior quality in design, products, and execution - a determining driver of Workspace Studio's positive momentum in recent years.

Last year, Workspace Studio demonstrated its ability to operationally manage large projects, covering tens of thousands of square meters, for offices with several thousand employees. These fit-outs were completed for renowned corporate clients such as OMV Petrom, Adobe Romania, Honeywell, EveryMatrix, and many others. The works included complete fit-out solutions, ergonomic furniture, flooring, partitions, acoustic solutions, and other architectural products from partners such as MillerKnoll, Impact Acoustic, Maars, and hundreds of other manufacturers with which the company collaborates for direct procurement, as well as turnkey fit-outs in which Workspace Studio managed the entire project.

2024 was defined by our ability to provide the best-performing solutions and services within our clients' budgets, while respecting our commercial promises. Orientation toward quality - in design, products, and execution - was a determining factor in growth. Companies' needs, whether we are talking about 100 or 1,000 employees, are essentially similar: supporting employees through physical and social ergonomics, design, and functionality. Only the scale of the projects varies, says Horatiu Didea, Managing Partner Workspace Studio.

The company fitted out more than 100,000 sqm of offices in 2024, managing large-scale projects with thousands of employees with agility, as well as more compact spaces. The projects required internal operational adaptations, demonstrating speed and efficiency in delivery without compromising attention to detail and high standards.

Workspace Studio also remained a partner for smaller businesses, offering efficiency and personalized solutions. The philosophy of budget centricity - delivering the highest quality within a given budget - was successfully applied in all collaborations, regardless of scale. From the design of collaborative spaces and the integration of booths and acoustic solutions, to adjustable desks and sustainable materials, Workspace Studio emphasized ergonomics, wellbeing, and flexibility.

The expansion of the portfolio with more than 20 new manufacturers, such as Maars for glass partitions, Kartell and Muuto for furniture, alongside the brands in the MillerKnoll family, strengthened the premium turnkey offer, delivering fit-outs with market-leading sustainability and quality certifications, all within optimized budgets as a direct distributor. These solutions reflect Workspace Studio's vision of the office that responds to current and future labor market trends, focused on adaptability to change, sustainability, and wellbeing.

The modern office stimulates innovation and collaboration

Post-pandemic, the office has transformed into a social destination, where employees connect and exchange ideas. It is also a space dedicated to informal learning, through mentorship and collaboration, extremely useful for younger generations or employees in professional development stages.

The modern office is a hub of productivity and connection, a catalyst for conversations that increase innovation and team cohesion. We are talking about design with modular areas, ergonomic furniture, optimized natural light, and high-performing acoustic solutions, all designed to attract talent and respond to current needs for wellbeing and sustainability, explains Horatiu Didea.

This wellbeing goes beyond physical comfort, integrating principles of inclusive design. Spaces are designed to be accessible and adaptable, including for neurodiversity, with solutions such as quiet focus areas, adjustable lighting, and ergonomic furniture, intended to support physical and mental health regardless of user profile.

Sustainability remains a priority, especially in the context of ESG regulations in force in the European Union. Workspace Studio uses recyclable materials, furniture with top environmental certifications, and strategies that reduce the carbon footprint of fit-outs.

The modern office must be energy efficient and environmentally responsible, and we deliver solutions that align companies' needs with ESG standards without sacrificing functionality or aesthetics, adds Horatiu Didea.

For 2025, the company maintains its commitment to high quality, sustainability, and human-centered design. Workspace Studio is investing more than EUR 300,000 in technology to accelerate deliveries and respond to a market that may be slower, but still has demand for efficient offices.

About Workspace Studio

With 15 years in the market, Workspace Studio distributes top furniture and architectural products, and is the sole MillerKnoll Certified Dealer in Romania. With more than 100,000 sqm fitted out in 2024, the company leads in premium turnkey solutions, integrating ergonomics and sustainability into modern offices for companies such as OMV Petrom, Unilever, Adobe Romania, Bimbo (Vel Pitar Group), UiPath, Honeywell, EveryMatrix, Profi, and many others.

Workspace Studio Group includes Workspace Studio and Allspace Interiors, specialized in ergonomic turnkey office fit-out solutions and distribution of architectural products, construction materials, and furniture.
