ADOBE 2022

ADOBE SYSTEMS ROMANIA Reimagining a Multi-Level Creative Hub

SCOPE
Client

Adobe Systems Romania, technology sector, large enterprise

Timeline

Completed June 2022 (full design + implementation)

Budget

Not disclosed (enterprise‑grade fitout)

Delivered

4,200 m² across multiple floors in Anchor Plaza, Bucharest

Key constraint

Designing a cohesive experience across five non‑contiguous floors while maintaining brand consistency and operational flow

Adobe needed to consolidate several teams into a single, multi‑level workspace inside Anchor Plaza, but the floors were separated and varied in geometry. The challenge was to create a unified spatial identity that supported collaboration, privacy, and the creative culture Adobe is known for—without disrupting ongoing operations during the phased implementation.

The core idea was to build a vertically connected workplace identity, using consistent material palettes, lighting logic, and branded touchpoints to make each floor feel like part of a single ecosystem. The design balanced open collaboration areas with enclosed focus rooms, ensuring acoustic comfort and flexibility for hybrid work.

From a delivery standpoint, the team sequenced construction and authorization floor by floor, minimizing downtime and allowing Adobe to maintain business continuity. Technical decisions—HVAC zoning, circulation flow, and modular furniture—were optimized for long‑term adaptability.

Unlike standard fitout approaches that treat each floor as an isolated project, this solution treated the entire 4,200 m² as one integrated organism, ensuring coherence in experience, efficiency, and brand expression.

THE OUTCOME
  • High adoption of collaborative areas and meeting rooms
  • Delivered on the planned timeline (June 2022)
  • Positive client feedback on spatial coherence and acoustic comfort
  • A workspace that continues to support team growth and hybrid workflows with minimal reconfiguration needs
THE DIFFERENTIATION

A typical competitor would have executed a floor‑by‑floor fitout with minimal cross‑level integration. Our approach created a unified, multi‑level creative hub, strengthening Adobe’s culture and ensuring long‑term functional resilience—an outcome only possible through holistic design thinking and tightly coordinated implementation.
