Why Workspace Studio

Choosing a workplace partner is a question of trust, clarity, and delivery capacity. Our role is to connect people, design, procurement, technical coordination, cost control, and long-term support into one coherent route.

Workspace Studio works with corporate clients across industries and project scales, from compact office interventions to complex headquarters. We combine experienced teams with direct access to premium furniture and architectural brands, giving each project a stronger technical and commercial foundation.

We stay close to the project beyond the first concept. Budget decisions, supplier choices, implementation logic, handover, service, warranty, and optimisation are treated as parts of the same responsibility.

Our people

Experienced solid team of cross industries professionals, including workplace strategists, designers, project managers, technical specialists, procurement experts, installation teams, and service support.

Integrated expertise at scale

700+ corporate clients, with projects from 100 m² to 30,000 m². The scale of our work allows us to understand different business needs, planning constraints, budgets, timelines, and implementation conditions.

Direct partnership with 500+ premium brands

We provide furniture and architectural solutions from the world’s best suppliers, selected for performance, durability, ergonomics, finish quality, and long-term value.

Cost predictability

Budget-centric design and transparent cost control help clients understand the commercial impact of decisions early, before they become site constraints or procurement compromises.

End to end delivery

A single point of contact from concept to handover keeps the process readable. Design intent, procurement, technical coordination, installation, and final adjustments stay connected.

Proven track record

15+ years on the market, with over 500,000 m² of corporate fitout delivered. Our experience is visible in the number of projects completed and in the consistency required to deliver at scale.

Operational strengths and long-term partnership

We offer strong project management, experienced technical teams, disciplined procurement, and execution capability at scale. We also stay involved after handover with service, warranty, optimisation, and support.
